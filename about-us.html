About US

The medical illustrator, an artist with advanced education in both science and visual communication, in collaboration with the scientist, the doctor and other specialists, analyzes and transforms complex information into images that are capable of speaking to the general public.

Extensive research, often observing a surgical procedure or a laboratory process, reading scientific papers and continuing communication with scientific collaborators -experts is an integral part of the creative work of the medical illustrator.
